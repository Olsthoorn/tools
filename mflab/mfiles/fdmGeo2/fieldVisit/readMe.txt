this directory/folder contains info on the field visit of May 6 2014 by the students doing the course geohydrology II (CIE 5440) of Water Management at TUDelft

Extra measurements were done and photos taken by Pierre Kamps and Theo Olsthoorn on May 7 and May 8.

These data and background information has been brought together into a report which is available on blackboard. This document should be considered a guide and reference. It all be used as a base to ask questions during the oral exam.

Modeling assignments are also with reference to this report.

Theo Olsthoorn May 21, 2014
